Insydin

Add Insydin - Our Official Music Bot with the feature of so many filters!

  • Music Cmds

  • Filters

  • Other Cmds

  • More Features

Music Commands

Music Commands - prefix : $, clear-queue, filter, loop, nowplaying, pause, play, queue, resume, search, shuffle, skip, stop, volume, w-filters.

  • You can also play music through youtube link.
  • You can also add youtube playlist in the queue.
  • You can increase the volume upto 100%

Insydin Filters

There are so many filters in insydin like 8D, gate, haas, phaser, treble, tremolo, vibrato, reverse, karaoke, flanger, mcompand, pulsator, subboost, bassboost, vaporwave, nightcore, normalizer, surrounding.

  • You can add so many filters at one time.
  • You can also check the filters applied on the song by "$filters" command.

Other Commands of the Bot

Other Commands - Connections, Ping, Invite.

  • You can check the bot ping by using "$ping" Command.
  • You can check the bots connections that how many vc's have insydin joined at that time.
  • You can get the youtube link, support server link & bots invite link through "$invite" command.

More Features

Insydin is stable and good music bot with quality sound and deepbass.

  • You can listen music in 8D sound.
  • You can add filters as much as possible in 1 song.
  • The Bot is 24/7 Online, it never goes down.
  • Insydin have Emojis interaction in every command or message.
